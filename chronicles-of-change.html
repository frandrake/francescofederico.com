Chronicles of Change

Where AI meets strategy. Weekly.

The Brief

AI moves fast. Most of the commentary doesn't keep up. This does.

Chronicles of Change is a weekly newsletter for senior leaders who need to separate signal from noise in enterprise AI. Each edition analyses one or two developments in depth—what happened, what it means strategically, and what to do about it. No roundups. No hype recaps.
