Francesco Federico is the Global Chief Marketing Officer at S&P Global, where he leads AI-powered marketing transformation across global financial markets. He is the author of The Agentic CMO, the strategic blueprint for marketing leaders navigating the transition from automation to autonomy through agentic AI. Francesco publishes Chronicles of Change, a weekly newsletter analysing enterprise AI developments for senior business leaders. A Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Marketing and member of the World Economic Forum's Strategic Communicators Exchange, he has held senior marketing and technology leadership roles at JLL, Acer, and Vodafone. His work focuses on designing hybrid human-AI organisations, marketing technology ecosystems, and AI governance frameworks.
